# Privacy Policy

Welcome to Budget PC Gaming HQ! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website, [Budget PC Gaming HQ]. We want to assure you that we are committed to safeguarding your data and being transparent about our practices.

**Information We Collect**

We collect limited information to improve your experience and provide you with relevant content. This may include:

*   **Log Data:** Like most websites, we automatically collect certain information when you visit our site. This includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring website, pages you visited, and the dates/times of your visit. This helps us understand how users navigate our site and improve its performance.
*   **Cookies:** We use cookies to personalize your experience and remember your preferences. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your ability to fully use our website.
*   **Email Address (If you subscribe):** If you choose to subscribe to our newsletter or contact us via email, we will collect your email address. We will only use your email address to send you relevant updates, promotions, and respond to your inquiries. You can unsubscribe from our email list at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in the email.
*   **Affiliate Links:** We use affiliate links to earn a commission when you purchase products through our recommendations. When you click on an affiliate link, the merchant may track your activity using cookies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these merchants and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

**How We Use Your Information**

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

*   **To Improve Our Website:** We analyze log data to understand how users interact with our website and identify areas for improvement.
*   **To Personalize Your Experience:** We use cookies to remember your preferences and provide you with a more personalized experience.
*   **To Communicate with You:** If you subscribe to our newsletter or contact us, we will use your email address to communicate with you.
*   **To Provide Relevant Content:** We use your information to provide you with content that is relevant to your interests.

**Data Sharing and Disclosure**

We will not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

*   **With Service Providers:** We may share your information with trusted service providers who assist us with operating our website, conducting our business, or providing services to you. These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
*   **When Required by Law:** We may disclose your information when required by law, such as in response to a subpoena or court order.

**Data Security**

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

**Your Rights**

You have the right to:

*   **Access your information:** You can request access to the information we hold about you.
*   **Correct your information:** You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
*   **Delete your information:** You can request that we delete your information.
*   **Opt-out of marketing communications:** You can unsubscribe from our email list at any time.

**Changes to This Privacy Policy**

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page and update the "Last Updated" date below.

**Contact Us**

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact form.
